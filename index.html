✦ Questions

Everything you need to know.

Choosing to send something across time is a big decision. We want you to feel completely certain before you do.

What happens to my message if AfterGlow closes down?
We hold all funds in a protected escrow account and work with a legal trustee. In the unlikely event we cease operations, your messages and funds are transferred to a designated partner service, and every recipient is notified. Your delivery will happen.
Can I edit or cancel a scheduled message?
Yes — you can update the recipient's details, delivery date, or message content at any time up to 30 days before the scheduled delivery. Cancellations made more than 90 days before delivery receive a full refund.
How do you handle "deliver when I'm gone" messages?
You designate a trusted notifier — a family member, lawyer, or executor — who contacts us when you pass. We can also integrate with estate legal processes. Once we receive verified confirmation, delivery is triggered within 30 days.
How are physical items stored?
All physical items are stored in climate-controlled, humidity-regulated archival facilities. Fragile items are wrapped in acid-free materials. We photograph and digitally catalogue everything upon receipt so you have a permanent record.
What if the recipient has moved or can't be reached?
We hold undeliverable items for up to 5 years and make three independent attempts to locate recipients. You can also provide alternate contact details. We never discard a message — we hold it until it finds its home.
Is my content private?
Completely. Digital content is end-to-end encrypted and only decrypted at the moment of delivery. Physical items are sealed and handled only by gloved staff. No one at AfterGlow reads your letters or views your videos.
